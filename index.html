About Us

Hey there! We're the team behind Milktea by JCFP, and we're thrilled to share our story with you.

Who We Are

Milktea by JCFP isn't just a brand; it's the brainchild of Jason, Cathy, Food, and Products (JCFP). We're a passionate group of individuals who share a love for creating exceptional culinary experiences.

Our Passion

At Milktea by JCFP, we pour our hearts into every cup of milk tea we serve. From selecting the finest ingredients to crafting unique flavor combinations, we're dedicated to delivering a delightful taste adventure with every sip.

Why Choose Us

What sets us apart? It's our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Whether you're craving a classic milk tea or feeling adventurous with one of our specialty blends, we've got something to tantalize your taste buds.

Get in Touch

Have a question or just want to say hello? We'd love to hear from you! Drop by our store or reach out to us via phone or email. We're here to make your milk tea experience extraordinary.

Thanks for being a part of the Milktea by JCFP family. Cheers to good times and great tea!
